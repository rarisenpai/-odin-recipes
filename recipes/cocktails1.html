Whiskey Sour


Descripition

The whiskey sour (aka bourbon sour) is a classic cocktail that’s on the list of
International Bartender Association’s IBA official cocktails.
This means it has an official definition.
Our version stays true to the original definition, with a few tweaks.
The ingredients for making a whiskey sour recipe are simple!
Here’s what you’ll need for this classic sour cocktail:

Ingredients

  • Bourbon whiskey
  • Lemon juice
  • Maple syrup or simple syrup
  • Garnish of orange peel and cocktail cherry

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon whiskey, lemon juice, and syrup to a cocktail shaker. Fill with a
    handful of ice and shake until very cold.
  2. Serve with ice, an orange peel and a cocktail cherry.
